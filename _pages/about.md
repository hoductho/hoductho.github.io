I work in **applied control for dynamical systems**, **robotics**, and **safe AI-enabled cognitive telerobotics**.

My research focuses on nonlinear, adaptive, and robust control; input shaping control; and robotic motion and interaction control. My most recent work studies stability-guaranteed learning methods for delay-robust, human-centered teleoperation with haptic feedback.
