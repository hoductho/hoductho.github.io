I work in **applied control for dynamical systems**, **robotics**, and **safe AI-enabled cognitive telerobotics**.

My research focuses on stability analysis, nonlinear/adaptive/robust control, robotic motion and force control, and stability-guaranteed learning methods for delay-robust human-centered teleoperation.
