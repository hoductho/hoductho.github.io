## Course Overview

This course provides students with fundamental knowledge of modern control theory for the analysis and design of complex control systems.

The course covers three main topics: nonlinear control, optimal control, and adaptive control. Topics include Lyapunov stability theory, feedback linearization, sliding mode control, calculus of variations, dynamic programming, LQR, Kalman filtering, LQG control, model reference adaptive control, self-tuning regulators, and gain scheduling.
